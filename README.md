# Pro_Weaponry  
**Forge your destiny — arm yourself with powerful melee weapons for Minetest!**  

Pro_Weaponry adds a selection of unique, beautifully balanced melee weapons to Minetest, along with crafting components to support their creation.  
Whether you favor speed or raw impact, this mod expands your combat options and gives players a new edge in battle.

---

## 🗡️ Features  
- Adds **seven distinct weapon types** to Minetest:  
  - Battlehammers  
  - Halberds  
  - Daggers  
  - Katanas  
  - Longswords  
  - Sabers 
  - Schytes 
- Each weapon has unique **combat stats** — reach, damage, speed, and durability.  
- Includes **miscellaneous crafting items** (handles, poles, bindings) for creating weapons.  
- Balanced for both **survival** and **PvP** gameplay.

---

## ⚙️ Requirements  
- **Minetest version:** 5.4.0 or newer  
- **Depends on:**  
  - `default` (from Minetest Game or compatible subgame)  
  - Optional: `wool` (included in Minetest Game)
